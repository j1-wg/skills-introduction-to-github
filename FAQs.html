FAQs - Mambo Yote Restaurant

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM from Monday to Sunday.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian dishes available on our menu.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer several gluten-free dishes. Please ask our staff for more details.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space available for our customers.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within a 10-mile radius of our restaurant.

Can I make a reservation?

Yes, you can make a reservation by calling us at (123) 456-7890 or through our website.

Do you have outdoor seating?

Yes, we have a beautiful outdoor seating area for our customers to enjoy.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, pets are allowed in our outdoor seating area.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for events and special occasions. Please contact us for more details.

Do you have a kids' menu?

Yes, we have a special menu for kids with a variety of options.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased at our restaurant or online.

Is there a dress code?

We have a casual dress code. However, we encourage our customers to dress smartly.

Do you have live music?

Yes, we have live music performances on weekends.

Do you offer Wi-Fi?

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for our customers.

Can I host a private event at your restaurant?

Yes, we have a private dining area available for events. Please contact us for more details.

Do you have vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegan dishes available on our menu.

Do you offer takeout?

Yes, we offer takeout services. You can place your order online or by calling us.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards our regular customers with discounts and special offers.

Do you have a bar?

Yes, we have a fully stocked bar with a wide selection of drinks.

Do you offer seasonal menus?

Yes, we offer seasonal menus that feature fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
